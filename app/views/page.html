Guidance:

The purpose of this app is to enable communication with subscribers, in case the usual methods do not work.

To send a message, you need to upload a list of email addresses. It needs to be a csv file, with only one column and no header row.
You can then type your message on the form below. It will be send immediately after you click 'Send'. The delivery statistics are available in gov.uk Notify account.

The list of subscribers is removed from the app automatically. It will be on the server for a maximum of 2h.
